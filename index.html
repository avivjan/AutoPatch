Auto-Patching: Enhancing Multi-Hop Reasoning in Language Models

Authors: Aviv Jan*, Daniel Tahory*, Or Talmi*, Or Abo Mokh*
(Independent Undergraduate Research Project)

Abstract:
We propose Auto-Patching, an automated hidden state intervention method to enhance multi-hop reasoning in LLMs. Our method systematically identifies and patches key states, improving model reasoning without hand-designed interventions.
